Our Story

Hand-It, A Unified Platform For NGO's

In 2023, four passionate college students—Ankita, Avdhut, Mandar, and Sharmeen—came together at their university in India, united by a shared vision: to empower NGOs across the country. Frustrated by the challenges faced by non-profits in gaining visibility and resources, they decided to create a unified platform called Hand-It.

Our Mission

At Hand-It, our mission is to empower non-governmental organizations (NGOs) across India by providing a unified platform that fosters collaboration, resource sharing, and community engagement

  • Enhancing Visibility
  • Facilitating Collaboration
  • Empowering Communities
